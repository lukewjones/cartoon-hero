Captain America

About Steve Rogers

Steve Rogers, also known as Captain America or Cap, was an American hero during WWII. While on a dangerous mission to stop the Red Skull, he was frozen under ice for 70 years. After he was found and taken out of the ice, he joined the Avengers and together they protect earth.
